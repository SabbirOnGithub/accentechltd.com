Offshore Development Center

A small scale offshore development center operates as an extension to client’s existing software engineering business. It strictly adheres to client’s business critical objectives while mutually considering the ideas, design validations, development processes, test plans and schedules for implementing the project under consideration.

Managed Resources

Client retains a team of engineers, managers or quality assurance personnel for a specific period and directly assigns tasks to the team. This is particularly useful when the client wants to augment their own development team with extra resources for a specific time-period or projects.

Fixed Price Application Development

Offshore team works with the clients to define their expected deliverable outcomes to determine a mutually agreed fixed price.
